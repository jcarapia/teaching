Video: Cultural Interaction*

For the next few minutes, you will watch a video as an observer watching two people from two cultures interact: Mawans and Lilos. The two people are meeting for the first time at a social event. Watch what happens. Notice how the two cultures act around each other, and make note of any differences you see regarding their methods of communicating.

When you are ready, begin the video and take notes on the interaction as best you can for the video’s duration.

Are you ready? Let’s begin! Start the video.
